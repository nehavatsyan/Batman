The One Man War On Crime

Although Batman now has a family of sorts comprised of Robin, Red Robin and Nightwing, among others, his origin as a one-man war on crime is perhaps one of the most endearing aspects of his character. if you've ever been a victim of a criminal, then you'll be familiar with the sense of rageful injustice that comes hand in hand. Bruce Wayne, as early as 8 years old, had suffered this injustice to such an extreme that he chose to arm himself with every weapon posiible and suit up to take back the treets, one lowlife at a time.

The Bat Cave

it houses literally the entire Bat-legacy from Batsuits to Batmobiles, as well as trophies of victory and oh, who could forget, a gaint T-Rex statue in fact the Batcave is conceptually similar to Bruce Wayne himself in many ways, where there once was a dark,endless void deep beneath the surface now resides the Batman. There are plenty of physical and metaphorical barriers that he places around himself amd his secret identity, but the Batcave is by far the most awesome.
